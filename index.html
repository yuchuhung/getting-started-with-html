Diaosi

For those who do not understand Chinese, "diaosi" refers to those who have no money, no house, does not have connections who reign the power in a group or community.

Those who just have a mediocre appearance also can be considered diaosi as well, those people usually need to work a lot harder than those who are not =(.

As you can see from the t-shirt on the left, they have limited resource around them to compete against those who have:

  • Money
  • Car
  • Appearance, look
  • Companion (women/men)
  • Authority
